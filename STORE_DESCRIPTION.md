WoHRedAlert is a read-only browser extension to help Ways of History players detect and get notified of incoming attacks. It operates by monitoring in-game state visible to the player and sending alerts to your Telegram or Discord account.

Key features:
- Real-time detection of incoming fleets and battles at your towns
- Notifications via Telegram Bot and Discord Webhook
- No automation or gameplay actions—100% read-only
- Respectful of privacy: tokens and webhooks only stored in your browser

Please note: this extension reads in-game data only for alerting, without any automated play controls. Usage is at your own risk and should comply with Ways of History's terms of service. We recommend requesting admin approval before use.

This tool was developed to improve accessibility for all players by providing asynchronous alerts to reduce constant screen monitoring requirements.
