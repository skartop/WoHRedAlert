# WoH Monitor Extension Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes what data the WoH Monitor extension collects, how it is used, and your rights.

## Data Collected

- **User-Provided Tokens:** Telegram bot API token, Telegram chat ID, and Discord webhook URL must be entered by the user for alert delivery.
- **In-Game State Data:** The extension reads game state (attack/fleet movements) from your Ways of History browser tab. This data is only accessible to you in your browser and is not sent or stored externally except for alert notifications.

## Data Usage

- The extension sends alert messages to your configured Telegram and/or Discord endpoints using the tokens/URLs you provide.
- No data is shared with any third parties beyond these configured alert services.
- No gameplay actions or data modifications are performed.

## Data Storage

- Your tokens and webhook URLs are stored locally in your browser's Chrome Storage Sync.
- No data is transmitted outside your local device except for alert API calls.

## User Rights

- You can view, update, or remove your tokens and webhooks in the extension settings at any time.
- You may disable or uninstall the extension to stop all data processing.

## Security

- The extension only uses secure HTTPS connections to Telegram and Discord APIs.
- Your tokens are never sent to any third party other than the configured alert endpoints.

## Disclaimer

This extension is a third-party tool created for personal alerting convenience. It is your responsibility to ensure its compliance with Ways of History terms of service.
